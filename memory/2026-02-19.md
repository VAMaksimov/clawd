## Key Regulatory Questions Answered

### ПДС (программа долгосрочных сбережений)
- Located in Chapter X.3 of Federal Law 75-FZ (Articles 36.38-36.50)
- Long-term savings program with state co-financing
- Up to 36,000₽/year government contribution matching
- 10-year minimum term

### NPF Compensation Structure
- Article 36.23 of Law 75-FZ
- Fixed: 0.5% of pension reserve value
- Variable: 15-20% of investment income above inflation

### Guarantee Contributions Payment Deadline (ASV)
- **Deadline: No later than April 15** of the year following the reporting year
- Source: Article 15, Part 10, Federal Law 422-FZ (December 28, 2013)
- Calculation basis: Average net assets × 0.975
- Rate: Minimum 0.0125% (can be increased to 0.025%, exceptionally to 0.05%)
- Payment from NPF's own funds to ASV account at Bank of Russia
